Dr. Disrespect
STATUS: Exposed, losing followers

Dr Disrespect or The Doc, is an American live streamer who is well know for playing popular fps and tps games. back in June 2022 he was banned for an undisclosed reason and started streaming on Youtube since. In June 2024, former Twitch employees disclosed that his ban was due to sexting with a minor using Twitch's Whisper feature in 2017. Dr Disrespect acknowledged the nature of his ban, and characterized the messages as "inappropriate". Following this Youtube has terminated their contract with Dr Disrespect.
